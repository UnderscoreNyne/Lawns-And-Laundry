What is FoodShare?

FoodShare is a B2B web application where food providers (restaurants, groceries, caterers, and community kitchens) create profiles, upload available meals/produce boxes with details (ingredients, allergens, portion size) and prices or donation flags, and manage customer orders. End-users (households, students, and community members) can browse items, view nutrition and pickup/delivery windows, request orders, pay or redeem vouchers, and receive real-time status updates. The platform acts as a marketplace for multiple organizations to offer discounted surplus meals and community food boxes across Toronto, supporting both sales and donation workflows.

Start by browsing our available food items or create an account to start sharing your surplus. You can also choose to login as a client or provider depending on your role.
